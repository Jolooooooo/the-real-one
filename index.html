Japan's Cherry Blossom

Every year, throughout the spring, Japan has cherry blossom season. The majority of cherry blossom celebrations start around the end of March and can extend well into May, however peak viewing times vary by location. When the flowering season starts and finishes is mostly determined by the weather.
